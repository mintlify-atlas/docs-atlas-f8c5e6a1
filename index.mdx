## Key Benefits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tax Compliance">
    Automatic NCF generation ensures compliance with Dominican tax regulations (DGII), including proper sequence management and cancellation tracking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real-Time Inventory">
    Every sale automatically adjusts inventory levels with full audit trails. Support for multiple warehouses and product tracking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Integrated Accounting">
    Sales automatically generate journal entries, receivables, and payment records using double-entry accounting principles.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Export Everything">
    Export sales, inventory movements, payments, and more to Excel with filtered data and custom columns.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
